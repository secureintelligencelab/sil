About Our Lab

Leading the way in cybersecurity innovation through cutting-edge research and collaboration

The Secure Intelligence Lab is a cutting-edge research facility at Crown Institute of Higher Education, dedicated to advancing the field of cybersecurity through innovative applications of artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Our interdisciplinary team of researchers, research assistants, and industry partners work collaboratively to address the most pressing security challenges of our digital age, developing novel solutions that protect critical infrastructure, personal data, and emerging technologies.

We focus on areas including threat detection, behavioral analytics, secure communication protocols, and AI-powered security systems that adapt to evolving cyber threats.
