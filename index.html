01 — About

A research lab built
around hard problems.

Leading cybersecurity innovation through interdisciplinary research and close collaboration with industry and academia.

The Secure Intelligence Lab is a research group at Crown Institute of Higher Education dedicated to advancing cybersecurity through novel applications of artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Our interdisciplinary team of researchers, research assistants, and industry partners works collaboratively to address the most pressing security challenges of the digital age — developing solutions that protect critical infrastructure, personal data, and emerging technologies.

We work across threat detection, behavioural analytics, secure communication protocols, and adaptive AI-powered security systems that evolve alongside the threats they defend against.

  • 01AI-Powered Cybersecurity — adaptive defence systems
  • 02Threat & Intrusion Detection — IoV, IoMT, networks
  • 03Machine & Deep Learning — explainable models
  • 04Blockchain Technology — privacy-preserving systems
  • 05Behavioural Analytics — keystroke & identity
