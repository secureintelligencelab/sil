Dr Md Imran Hossain

Biography

Dr Md Imran Hossain is a Research Associate at the Secure Intelligence Lab. He completed his PhD in IT at UNSW.

His expertise lies in blockchain technology and cybersecurity, contributing to the lab's work on secure, privacy-preserving distributed systems.

Research Interests

  • Blockchain technology
  • Cybersecurity and secure systems
  • Distributed and privacy-preserving architectures

Role in the Lab

As Research Associate, Imran contributes to active research projects and the development of the lab's blockchain and security prototypes.
